Tweaks:

Merriam

Merriam is a paid tweak that allows the customisation of your whole device.

Versions: 12 to 14.

Price: 0.99

Colouration

Colouration is a paid tweak that allows the customisation of every colour of your device.

Versions: 12 to 14.

Price: 0.99

ApolloColouring

ApolloColouring is a tweak to allow the customisation of the apollo app.

Versions: 12 to 14

Price: free

WidgetCustoms

WidgetCustoms is a tweak that allows you to customise ios 14 widgets.

Versions: 14 +

Price: free

CarPlayPasscode

CarPlayPasscode is a tweak to enable carplay passcode.

Versions: IOS 12 to 14

Price: free

FastCC

FastCC is a tweak to speed up the opening of the control center on IOS.

Versions: 12 to 14

Price: free

GoAwaySep

GoAwaySep is a tweak to remove system seperator lines.

Versions: IOS 12 to 14

Price: free

TappyKeyboard

TappyKeyboard is an IOS tweak to add haptics or taptics to your keyboard touches.

Versions: 12 to 14

Price: free
