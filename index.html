Tweaks:

Merriam

  • Merriam is a paid tweak that allows the customisation of your whole device. Reaching almost 200 features Merriam is a cheap way to customise every aspect of your IOS device.

  • Versions: IOS 12 to 14+.

  • Price: 0.99

Colouration

  • Colouration is a paid tweak that allows the customisation of every colour of your device.This tweak is a cheap way to safely colour most elements on your IOS device.

  • Versions: IOS 12 to 14+.

  • Price: 0.99

ApolloColouring

  • ApolloColouring is a tweak to allow the customisation of the apollo app.

  • Versions: IOS 12 to 14+

  • Price: FREE

WidgetCustoms

  • WidgetCustoms is a tweak that allows you to customise ios 14 widgets.

  • Versions: 14+ only

  • Price: FREE

CarPlayPasscode

  • CarPlayPasscode is a tweak to enable the carplay passcode view on your IOS device.

  • Versions: IOS 12 to 14+

  • Price: FREE

FastCC

  • FastCC is a tweak to speed up the opening of the control center on IOS.

  • Versions: IOS 12 to 14+

  • Price: FREE

GoAwaySep

  • GoAwaySep is a tweak to remove system seperator lines.

  • Versions: IOS 12 to 14+

  • Price: FREE

TappyKeyboard

  • TappyKeyboard is an IOS tweak to add haptics or taptics to your keyboard touches.

  • Versions: IOS 12 to 14+

  • Price: FREE
